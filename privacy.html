Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Code Aeternum respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our software and website.

2. Data Collection in Extensions

Our browser extensions (including TG Media Grabber Pro) are designed with privacy first. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal browsing data, messages, or media content to our servers.

All processing of media files happens locally on your device within the browser environment.

3. Donations

Our extensions are free to use. If you choose to support development via donation platforms (such as Ko-fi), those transactions are processed entirely by the third-party platform. Code Aeternum does not receive or store your payment details.

4. Feedback Data

If you submit feedback through our extension, the message content and feedback type are sent to a Google Form. No personal identifiers (name, email, IP) are attached to this submission unless you voluntarily include them in the message.

5. Website Analytics

Our website uses standard, non-invasive analytics to understand traffic sources. We do not use this data to profile individuals.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at codeaeternum@outlook.com.
