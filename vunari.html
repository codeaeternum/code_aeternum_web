Built around the way families actually play

🃏

Sietes with Family Rules

Play on one device, against bots, or with nearby iPhones and iPads. Configurable rules include the family limit that gets every seven onto the table within four rounds.

🤝

Nearby Play

Create a private table and join from another device on the local network. The core game does not depend on a public server.

📒

Clear Table Ledger

Give every player a different starting balance, record additional contributions, and preserve results across repeated rounds at the same table.

↩️

Reconnect and Resume

Sietes tables are designed to recover after the app closes or a device disconnects, preserving seats, balances, and the current round.

🀄

Conquián for Two

Share one device safely: each hand stays covered until the correct player confirms it is their turn.

🔒

Private by Default

Your nickname, settings, tables, and game history stay on your device or are exchanged directly with the devices you choose; they do not pass through a Vunari server.

From pocket to table in three steps

1

Choose a Game

Open Sietes or Conquián and select how your family wants to play.

2

Set the Table

Use points or your own denomination, give each person a starting balance, and confirm the rules.

3

Keep the Story

Play again with the same running balance or close the table with a result everyone can review.

A scoreboard, not a payment service

Vunari records the amounts chosen by your group. It does not sell or redeem points, accept deposits, transfer money, or award cash prizes.
